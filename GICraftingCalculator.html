Genshin Impact Crafting Calculator

Calculates how much of one material you could make in Genshin Impact. Fill in the empty slots with how many you have of an item. For example, if you wanted to see how many Shivada Jade Chunks you could make with what you have, you would fill in how many you currently have of each rarity and then you would see how many you could get if you crafted all of your Shivada Jade gems of all rarities into into Shivada Jade Chunks.
